About US

Established in 1978 Nehru World School is an English medium co-educational School affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (C.B.S.E.).NWS is the beginning of a lifelong adventure known as learning. The School nurtures a spirit that fosters children's natural inquisitiveness and delight in learning. The School provides each student, an international and exciting educational experience designed to become a caring, thoughtful, principled and well-balanced human being. Students will have opportunities to become technologically literate, learn to communicate effectively, and take risks in a supportive environment. Nehru World School received International School Award from the British Council in 2011 for successfully embedding internationalism in their curriculum. The School has a very strong Continuous Professional Development wing and regularly participates in Community Outreach programmes. In 2013, NWS was amongst the first schools in the country to offer CBSE I (International) Curriculum.




Admissions

In assessing the suitability of a candidate, our admission staff reviews each applicant's complete application. The applicant's prospects for success are evaluated thoroughly by at least three experienced staff members working independently of the other. This reliable and responsive process has, over the years, enabled us to know a great deal about our students before they enroll, which facilitates our planning for their future integration into the school community. We follow a three step system to grant admissions:

Step 1: Get a school prospectus and application package from the School or Online

Step 2: Submit Hard copy of the form along with the required documents to the school

Step 3: If admission is granted, submit fees and required documents at the specified date

Infrastructure

The Campus

Nehru World School is spread over a campus of about two acres. The junior and senior blocks are built around a large playground and open assembly place with two national level basketball courts. In addition to regular classrooms, the buildings include five science laboratories, three computer laboratories, visual art and music studios, 2 Audio Visual Rooms, a state-of-the-art facility for videoconferencing and a well-stocked library. The Pre-Primary and Primary wing of Nehru World School are Multiple Intelligence(s) enabled and housed within the school is a ‘MI Resource Centre’ equipped with teaching aids to encourage holistic development.
All classrooms and staffrooms of the School are equipped with Air Conditioners to maintain ambient temperature at all times.

Computing Facilities

Information Technology is an indispensable tool in education and NWS is committed to effective use of technology in the regular classroom. All computer labs are connected to our Intra school network and also have broadband access. In addition to regular computer labs, three laptop trollys are also available for classroom use.


3D in Education

NWS was the first school in India to set up a 3D (Three Dimensional) Lab on its premises. Through this technology, students are able to see complex concepts in 3D and understand them better.In this lab students put on 3D glasses and the lessons come alive in front of their eyes. They visualise chemical reactions floating in the room, see organs of the body as they carry out their usual daily functions or take journeys through 3D virtual worlds.


Classrooms

We offer two classroom sizes which are 20 X 20ft. and 20 X 30ft. Student strength varies from 25-35 in each classroom. The intention is to have a layout that supports and enhances teaching- learning while keeping it esthetically pleasing and functional. Every classrooms at NWS is Air-Conditioned and is equipped with an Interactive Board, AV equipment and big display areas for student work.



Co-Curricular areas

Integrated Approach

NWS complements a strong academic foundation with a rich selection of co-curricular activities designed to enhance a student's all round development and cultivate varied interests. The School's co-curricular programme provides many opportunities for students to get involved in arts, athletics, and student clubs


Personality Development

We demonstrate our commitment to each student's social, physical and emotional growth through our Life Skills program, an age-appropriate and focused sequence for Kinder garten classes through class XII. We inspire our students to become good public speakers and self-advocates, to have healthy bodies and body image, and to be empathic, effective communicators. We have developed a social and emotional support system that adapts to our students needs


Curricular Structure

Our curriculum is truly progressive & learner-friendly. The school provides a comprehensive academic curriculum supported by a system of instructions that encourages children to be logical, analytical, creative & communicative. We ensure overall development of children which does not depend on rote method and where the classroom teaching is flexible and integrated to the syllabus.


Sports & Games

Extending education beyond the confines of the classroom, the School cultivates values of team spirit, discipline and healthy competition through a variety of sports activities including skating, athletics, kho-kho, badminton, basketball, handball, physical fitness, chess, table tennis, cricket, volleyball & yoga.


Academic Areas

School Curriculum

The school is affiliated to CBSE as well as CBSEi and follows approved courses of studies from Pre-Primary up to class XII. The curriculum is well planned, graded & innovative based on CBSE & Continuous Comprehensive Education (CCE) guidelines.


Study Material

We follow the books prescribed by CBSE. Clear cut objective to prescribe these books is to maintain the universality by the students of our schools with the students beyond.The syllabus is enriched by well-structured lesson plans, teacher designed worksheets and lesson specific activities ensuring that the students are equipped with knowledge, skills and values.


Scholarships

Acknowledging the efforts put in by students and their families in securing outstanding results in the school's internal and CBSE examinations, Nehru World School awards several merit based scholarships every year.
